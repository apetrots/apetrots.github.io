Scavenger
Dev Log #1: An Introduction
3/8/21 - 3/14/21

Scavenger is a pretty self-explanatory title for a 2D platformer game I'm developing. The core loop of the game is just that: scavenging in a world that has undergone extensive destruction following their technological peak. The destruction is not total, and there are still remnants of old civilization, but they have morphed and changed in wild, near unrecognizable ways.
